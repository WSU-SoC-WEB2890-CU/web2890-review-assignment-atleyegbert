Great web design for me, lies in a foundation built on empathy and understanding. Accessibility takes center stage at every stage of the design process. Continuous improvement is integral through redesigning and reworking. I strive to create designs that not only meet but exceed expectations.

Designed with Empathy

In the web design process, I consider empathy to be the cornerstone of creating meaningful and user-centric experiences. Understanding the diverse perspectives, needs, and challenges of users is essential in crafting designs that resonate and connect. Actively listening to users is not just a step in the process but an ongoing opportunity for growth and improvement. It opens the door to valuable insights and allows for a dynamic response to evolving user expectations.

Accessibility at Every Stage

The importance of accessibility in web design cannot be overstated. I believe accessibility should be carefully considered and integrated at every stage of the design process. Beyond meeting legal requirements, embracing accessibility fosters a more inclusive and positive user experience. It reflects a commitment to equal access to information and services, promoting a digital environment that embraces diversity and empowers all users.

Alwaying Improving

To me, the key to success lies in a commitment to constant improvement. Recognizing that the first iteration is rarely the final destination, I approach design as an iterative process that requires constant evaluation and refinement. Redesigning and reworking become integral components, allowing for the incorporation of new insights, user feedback, and emerging trends. Each iteration is an opportunity for enhancement, an evolution that moves the design closer to its full potential. Embracing this key component of design helps me to remain adaptable to the ever-changing needs of users.

A breif introduction to my recent projects:

I created this website as an example of the knowledge and experience I have gained through my studies of web accessibility. This site is fully compliant with the standards outlined by the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.1 Level AA. Testing was conducted to guarantee compatibility with both keyboard navigation and screen readers. My commitment to accessibility goes beyond compliance, I am aiming to create a platform where everyone can engage with the content effortlessly and enjoy a fully accessible online environment.
Visit My Accessibility Website

I had the chance to evaluate the accessibility features of the "Cotton On USA" online retail website. My assessment involved utilizing the WAVE tool, an online web accessibility checker, and performing tests to ensure compatibility with screen readers and keyboard navigation. The outcomes of these tests were consolidated into a comprehensive report that outlines each identified issue, categorized based on the time required for resolution and the level of urgency for addressing them.
